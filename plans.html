Frequently Asked Questions

What is Purple Squirrel Media?
Purple Squirrel Media is a next-generation streaming platform that combines traditional content delivery with Web3 features like NFT-gated content, creator monetization tools, and interactive engagement through our proprietary INSIDE™ toolkit.
What are NFT-gated exclusives?
NFT-gated content is exclusive material that can only be accessed by holders of specific NFTs. This creates unique collectible access passes for premium content, live events, and creator exclusives. Connect your Solana wallet to verify ownership and unlock content.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. There are no cancellation fees or long-term commitments.
What is the INSIDE™ toolkit?
The INSIDE™ toolkit includes proprietary engagement features: Inside-Chat™ for live discussions during streams, Inside-Polls™ for real-time audience interaction, Inside-Ad™ for non-skippable monetization with 100% genuine impressions, and Inside-Analytics™ for creators to track their content performance.
How does creator revenue sharing work?
Squirrel Creator plan members receive 70% of all revenue generated from their content, including subscriptions, NFT sales, and ad revenue. Payments are processed monthly via traditional methods or crypto (SOL, USDC).
What devices are supported?
Purple Squirrel Media is available on web browsers, iOS, Android, smart TVs (coming soon), and streaming devices. Our responsive design ensures a great experience on any screen size.
