Frequently Asked Questions

Why pay with Solana?
Solana payments are instant, secure, and cost almost nothing (~$0.00025 per transaction). Unlike credit cards that charge 2-3% fees, we pass the savings to you. Plus, you get an NFT membership token.
What wallet do I need?
We recommend Phantom wallet - it's free, secure, and works in your browser. Download it from phantom.app and fund it with SOL from any exchange.
Can I pay with USDC instead?
Yes! Toggle to USDC above for stable pricing. USDC is a stablecoin pegged to USD, so you'll always pay the exact dollar amount shown.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are month-to-month with no commitment. Simply don't renew next month. Your NFT membership token remains yours to keep or trade.
What happens to my NFT if I cancel?
You keep your NFT forever! It just won't provide active subscription benefits until renewed. Original holder perks remain active.
