Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Purple Squirrel Media ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our streaming platform and NFT membership services.

Privacy Highlights:
  • We collect minimal personal data - primarily your wallet address
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • Blockchain transactions are public and permanent
  • You can use Free TV without providing any information

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Wallet Address: Your Solana wallet address when you connect to access premium features
  • Contact Information: Email address if you contact support or sign up for updates
  • Creator Content: If you're a Creator member, content you upload

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Content viewed, watch history, streaming quality preferences
  • IP Address: For geographic content restrictions and security
  • Cookies: Session data, preferences, analytics

1.3 Blockchain Data

  • Transaction History: NFT purchases and transfers are recorded on the Solana blockchain
  • NFT Ownership: Your membership status is verified on-chain
  • Public Data: Blockchain data is publicly accessible and permanent

2. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used Legal Basis
Verify membership access Wallet address, NFT ownership Contract performance
Stream content Device info, IP address Contract performance
Personalize experience Watch history, preferences Legitimate interest
Prevent fraud IP, device fingerprint Legitimate interest
Analytics Aggregated usage data Legitimate interest
Customer support Email, wallet address Contract performance

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Oracle, Azure), analytics, CDN providers
  • Blockchain Networks: Transactions are processed on Solana (public blockchain)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Session management, authentication
  • Preference Cookies: Language, playback settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Understanding usage patterns

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work properly.

5. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your wallet holds a valid NFT
  • Watch History: 2 years, then anonymized
  • Support Tickets: 3 years
  • Blockchain Data: Permanent (we cannot delete on-chain data)

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (except blockchain records)
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@purplesquirrel.media

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access to data

However, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for securing your wallet.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us immediately.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our servers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to know what data we collect
  • Right to delete data
  • Right to opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes through our platform or email.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions:

Email: privacy@purplesquirrel.media
Address: Purple Squirrel Media, Privacy Team

Note: This policy applies to purplesquirrel.media and our associated services. Third-party links on our platform have their own privacy policies.
