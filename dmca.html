DMCA & Copyright Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Purple Squirrel Media respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects our users to do the same. This policy outlines our procedures for addressing copyright infringement claims in accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

1. Notification of Infringement

If you believe that content on our platform infringes your copyright, please send a written notice containing:

  1. A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized agent
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed
  3. Identification of the infringing material and its location on our platform (URL)
  4. Your contact information (address, phone number, email)
  5. A statement that you have a good faith belief the use is not authorized
  6. A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information is accurate and you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner
Send DMCA Notices To:

Email: dmca@purplesquirrel.media
Subject Line: DMCA Takedown Notice

Mailing Address:
Purple Squirrel Media
DMCA Agent
[Address to be added]

2. Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notification containing:

  1. Your physical or electronic signature
  2. Identification of the removed material and its former location
  3. A statement under penalty of perjury that you believe the material was removed by mistake
  4. Your name, address, phone number, and email
  5. A statement consenting to jurisdiction of federal court in your district
  6. A statement that you will accept service of process from the complainant

3. Repeat Infringer Policy

We will terminate access for users who are repeat infringers:

  • First offense: Warning and content removal
  • Second offense: Temporary suspension
  • Third offense: Permanent ban (NFT may be revoked from platform access)
Warning: Filing a false DMCA notice or counter-notification is perjury and may result in legal liability. Only submit notices if you have a good faith belief of infringement or error.

4. Creator Content Guidelines

If you're a Creator tier member uploading content:

  • You must own or have rights to all content you upload
  • Do not upload copyrighted music, videos, or images without permission
  • Properly license any third-party content
  • You are responsible for any infringement claims against your content

5. Free TV Channels

Our Free TV section aggregates publicly available streams. If you own content appearing in these channels:

  • Contact us to verify your ownership
  • We will remove unauthorized streams promptly
  • Alternatively, we can discuss licensing arrangements

6. NFT and Copyright

Important clarifications about our NFT memberships:

  • Purchasing an NFT does NOT transfer copyright of content
  • NFTs grant viewing access, not ownership of media
  • Recording or redistributing content is prohibited
  • NFT artwork is licensed, not sold (you may display but not commercialize)

7. Response Time

We aim to respond to valid DMCA notices within:

  • 24-48 hours: Acknowledgment of receipt
  • 5-7 business days: Content removal if claim is valid
  • 10-14 business days: Counter-notification review

8. Contact

For copyright-related questions that are not formal DMCA notices:

copyright@purplesquirrel.media

Note: This policy applies to content hosted directly on our platform. For content on third-party Free TV channels, contact the source broadcaster.
